Add Funds to Your Wallet

Why is Having a Wallet Important?

Having a wallet simplifies your food ordering process by allowing you to pre-load funds, which eliminates the need for on-the-spot payments and speeds up the checkout process.

  • Convenient and Fast Transactions
  • No Need to Carry Cash
  • Easily Track Your Spending
  • Avoids Transaction Failures

How to Add Funds to Your Wallet

Step 1: Log In

Ensure you are logged in to your account.

Step 2: Navigate to Wallet

Go to the Wallet section from the navigation menu.

Step 3: Enter Amount

Specify the amount you wish to add to your wallet and click 'Add to Wallet'.

Step 4: Confirm

Confirm the transaction to complete the process.
